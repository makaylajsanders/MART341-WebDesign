Taco Turkey Skillet

  • 1lb of turkey
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1 can of corn
  • 3 cups of rice
  • shredded cheese
  • sour cream

Directions

  1. Grind up turkey in pan
  2. Add black beans, corn, green peppers, and rice to pan
  3. While the veggies are heating up, preheat the oven to 425oF.
  4. Add a layer of cheese on top of the skillet, and place in the oven for 5 minutes to melt the cheese
  5. After the layer of cheese is melted, it is ready to be served!
