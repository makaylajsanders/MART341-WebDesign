Why did I choose this project?

I am going to try this recipe for Thanksgiving this year! I love combining fruit and spicy foods together. I also love dips at parties. It is a great way to share a lot of different foods!




What advice do I have for beginners?

Be patient! Waiting for something in the fridge can make time go slow. This recipe takes some time, but is SO worth the wait!
