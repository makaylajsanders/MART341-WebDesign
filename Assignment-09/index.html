Cranberry Jalepeño Cream Cheese Dip

Instructions

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fresh, uncooked cranberries
  • 1/4 cup green onion
  • 1-2 fresh jalepeño peppers
  • 2 Tbsp. cilantro
  • 3/4 to 1 cup sugar
  • 1 Tbsp. lemon juice
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 16oz cream cheese (whipped)



Instructions

Use hand food chopper to chop cranberries. Chop green onion, jalapeño peppers, an cilantro. In a medium sized bowl, add chopped cranberries, green onion, cilantro, and jalapeños. Add sugar, lemon juice, and salt on top of cranberry mixture, and stir gently until blended. Then, cover with plastic wrap and place in refrigerator and stir all ingredients together. Strain out all liquid using a collander with small holes. Next, whip softened cream cheese with hand mixer until smooth (takes about 2 minutes.) Spread cream cheese over bottom of a pie plate or 9x9 dish. Next step would be to pour the cranberry mixture atop cream cheese and keep in refrigerator until ready to serve. Enjoy with Ritz crackers!

Hope you enjoy this recipe!




Don't Forget the Most Important Steps!

Refigerate overnight AND strain the cranberries BEFORE you put them on the cream cheese! You don't want it to be runny! Enjoy!
